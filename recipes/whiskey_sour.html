Whiskey Sour

Description

The whiskey sour is a mixed drink containing whiskey, lemon juice, sugar, and optionally, a dash of egg white. It is a type of sour, a mixed drink with a base spirit, citrus juice, and a sweetener.

Ingredients

  • Whiskey - 40 ml
  • Lemon juice - 40 ml
  • Sugar syrop - 30 ml
  • Angostura - 5 ml
  • Orange slices - as a garnish

Steps

  1. Fill the shaker with ice
  2. Pour whiskey to the shaker
  3. Add lemon juice
  4. Add sugar syrop
  5. Add some angostura drops
  6. Close the shaker and shake it for 20-30 seconds.
  7. Fill the glass with ice
  8. Pour the alcohol from the shaker to glass using bartender strainer
  9. Add a few slices of orange as a garnish
  10. Done!
